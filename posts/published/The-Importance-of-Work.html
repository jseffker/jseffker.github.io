The spider spins webs. The beaver builds dams. Bees pollinate flowers. Trees give fruit and block the sun's rays. God gave each creature a job, but it's not just busywork -- it helps others. These creatures didn't choose their job, but they did have to find it. What's your job? God will help you find it. How can we find our "job"? Serviceful work should have a positive impact on society. In addition to this, try to find things that... - you enjoy. - you're capable of doing, and maybe even excel at. - you do naturally -- that is, you find you're able to return to it often and work at it consistently. What some of the blessings God gives us through serviceful work? - fulfillment and fun in our lives - distraction from some discomforts - relaxation for our mind - exercise for our body
